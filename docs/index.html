Welcome to Lilies in the Garden.

Located in small town Spartansburg, PA, Lilies in the Garden is an aspiring flower business. We grow lilies, irises, and a variety of other beautiful flowers on our property and simply wish to share our love for flowers. As our plants spread, we will be separating them to create room for more. As these separated plants become available, we will post photos and prices on our Facebook page, which you can find here.

Feel free to message our Facebook page with any inquiries.
