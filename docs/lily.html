This gallery displays the lilies we currently have growing. Some of these were purchased, some traded, and others have simply always grown on the property. Some names are registered names from breeders, others are unofficial garden names we assigned to ones we couldn't identify. Since our site is new, these images are from last year and are not of the best quality. We will be updating them as our flowers bloom throughout the year. If you know the proper registered name for one of our lilies please let us know! Check our facebook for available flowers here.
