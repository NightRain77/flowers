This gallery displays the lilies we currently have growing. Some of these were purchased, some traded, and others have simply always grown on the property. Some names are registered names from breeders, others are unofficial garden names we assigned to ones we couldn't identify. Registered flowers will have a link to a webpage that provides information on the specific flower. If you know the proper registered name for one of our unidentified lilies please let us know! Images that do not belong to us are because the flower has not yet bloomed in our yard. The images will be replaced with our own once our plant blooms. Check our facebook for available flowers here.
